Things About me:

My name is Brooke Nelson I am from Texas. I love to read books if I could I would prefer to never go anywhere and read all day. But I also love to go hiking and be out with my friends. I cant really decide which I like better if I am being honest. Most days I have a battle with myself on whether to go out and play or to stay in and read. Sometimes when I cant decide I do both at the same time.

How I got into this major:

When I first came to collage my dad said that I should be an electrical engineer. And so being the great daughter that I am I was like sure why not! I started to take some classes and took a programming class and fell in love with programming. Changed my major over to Computer Science.
